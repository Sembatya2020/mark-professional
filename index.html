My Approach

Following the Nature of Process

The spirit of Processwork follows the nature of people, relationships, groups, and world situations to help unfold unique ways we can live. We find creative responses to conflicts, losses, big changes, and personal or collective challenges.

Informed by ecology, field theory, non-duality, subtle energies, Dreambodywork, tantric practice, attachment, and trauma, I see us as part of nature—not separate from our deepest natures.

We always tailor the process to exactly where you are, guided by your feedback, energy, edges, and hesitations. Bodywork has been profoundly helpful in my own journey through loss and grief, so I now weave deeper somatic and energy practices into my work.

Heartful presence remains central to both my practice and life—shaped by my own periods of profound change—while staying open to how diversity unfolds in you and your relationships.

My work is informed by wide ranging areas such as nature/ecology, trauma, dreaming, non-duality, attachment, child development, indigenous wisdom, intersectionality and the 'Social Graces' *.

Methodology

How We Work

In Processwork, the Dreambody reveals depths of information and wisdom in many forms. The process unfolds through precisely attending to signals and information arising in our work together.

My approach flows verbally and through movement, dreaming, visualisation, sound/music, somatic experience in the body, and subtle energy body.
